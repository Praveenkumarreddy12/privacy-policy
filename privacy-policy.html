Privacy Policy – Face of Fitness Challenge

Effective Date: June 21, 2025

VE Commercial Vehicles (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you participate in the Face of Fitness challenge organized by VE Commercial Vehicles.

1. What Data We Collect

When you participate in the challenge, we may collect the following information:

  • Google Account Information: Name and email ID used to log in and authorize data sharing.
  • Fitness Data: Daily step count synced from your Google Fit account.
  • Participation Metadata: Timestamp of each step count sync for leaderboard tracking.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used only for the following purposes:

  • To track and verify your participation in the Face of Fitness challenge.
  • To generate daily, weekly, and final leaderboards within your Area Office.
  • To identify and recognize the top performers fairly.
  • To communicate challenge updates or results (via email if needed).

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal data with any third-party services or vendors.

Data access is strictly limited to internal team members managing the challenge within VE Commercial Vehicles.

Leaderboard rankings may be visible to other participants within your Area Office, but only limited information (Name, Step Count) is displayed.

4. Data Retention

We will retain your step data and email ID only for the duration of the challenge and up to 30 days after its conclusion, solely for result validation and reward processing.

After that, all personal and activity data will be permanently deleted from our records.

5. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement reasonable safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. This includes encrypted storage where applicable and limited access control.

6. Your Consent

By joining the Face of Fitness challenge and authorizing access to your Google Fit data, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

You may revoke your data access at any time through your Google account settings; however, doing so will remove you from leaderboard participation.
